Corvus Zip Tie Installer

Automated Wiring Harness Tool

Project Overview

The Corvus Zip Tie Installer is a mechanical solution designed for automating the installation of zip ties on aerospace wiring harnesses. This tool streamlines the manufacturing process by improving precision and reducing human error.


My Role

I led the mechanical design using CAD software, evaluated motor requirements, and tested the physical system with harness mockups. Collaborated with electrical and software teams to integrate automated controls.


Technologies Used

  • SolidWorks for mechanical design
  • 3D printing and laser-cut prototyping
  • Arduino-based motor control
  • Mechanical testing and iteration
