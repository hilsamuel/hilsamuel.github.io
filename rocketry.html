OSU High Power Rocketry

Experimental and Competitive Rocket Design

Project Overview

Oklahoma State University's high-powered rocketry team desings, constructs, and tests several high-performance launch vehicles. Developed for competitive and research-driven missions, these rockets integrate propulsion, avionics, and structural systems under strict performance constraints.


My Role

I worked on both the manufacturing and propulsion sub-teams. My efforts included machining custom components, assembling and winding carbon fiber airframes, mixing APCP solid rocket propellant, and performing motor integration tasks.


Technologies & Tools Used

  • SolidWorks and OpenRocket for design and simulation
  • CNC machining and composite layups
  • Experimental solid motor formulation and testing
